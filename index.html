Explore Our Roots and Traditions

Pamiri culture is rich with history, language, and tradition, rooted deeply in the majestic mountains of the Pamirs. The Pamirs, often called "The Roof of the World," are located in the eastern part of Tajikistan, bordering Afghanistan, China, and Kyrgyzstan. The region has been inhabited for over 2,500 years and is known for its unique culture, language, and history.

Where is Pamir Located?

The Pamirs are located in the eastern part of Tajikistan, bordering Afghanistan, China, and Kyrgyzstan. This region is known for its high-altitude mountain ranges, serving as a historical crossroads for ancient traders.
